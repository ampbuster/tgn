## The 5-Part Framework

### 1. The Tonnage Illusion

When we talk about "200-300 million tonnes," we are talking about **tonnes of mineralized rock (dirt)**, not pure tungsten.

- Tungsten deposits are typically very low grade (0.1% to 0.3% WO₃)
- 300 million tonnes of rock = 300,000 to 600,000 tonnes of actual WO₃ metal
- Need massive, multi-hundred-million-dollar processing plant
- Takes years to build

### 2. JORC Hierarchy (Why "Verified" Doesn't Mean "Bankable")

In Western mining (under JORC or NI 43-101 standards), there is a strict legal hierarchy:

**RESOURCE (Geologically Verified):**
- Geologists have drilled wide-spaced holes
- Verified the metal is there
- **Banks will NOT lend a single dollar to build a mine based on this**
- Considered too risky

**RESERVE (Economically Mineable):**
- Engineers, metallurgists, and environmental lawyers have proven
- Exactly how to extract, process, and legally permit at a profit
- **Banks WILL lend on this**
- The "bankable" stage

**The Critical Distinction:**
Most U.S. "verified" tungsten is stuck at the **Resource** stage. To turn a Resource into a Reserve requires:
- US$20M-US$50M in spending
- 5-7 years of work
- Geotechnical drilling, water studies, metallurgical testing
- Modern NEPA-compliant permits

**High prices incentivize companies to *start* this process today, but it guarantees the supply won't hit the market until the 2030s.**

### 3. The "Brownfield Illusion" (Why Old U.S. Mines Are Actually Greenfield)

A true **Brownfield** project means the infrastructure (roads, power, water rights, processing mills) and the legal permits are still active and intact.

**Historical U.S. Tungsten Mines Are NOT Brownfield — 2015-2024 "Tombstones" Era, 2026+ DFARS Active Ramp:**

**The Permitting Reset (The NEPA Trap):**
