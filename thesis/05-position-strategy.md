## Stop-Loss Strategy

### Stop-Loss: A$0.12 (Catastrophic Insurance)

**This is NOT gain protection. This is INSURANCE.**

**What it protects against:**
- TGN bankruptcy / administration
- Project completely fails
- Total loss scenario (A$0.05 or lower)

**What it does NOT protect against:**
- Normal volatility (50% drawdowns in mining stocks)
- Thesis being right but stock staying flat
- Slow re-rating (12-24 months)

**Expected fill at A$0.10-0.11:**
- 10-20% slippage due to illiquidity
- Total loss scenario: ~A$10-11k (vs A$22.5k current value)
- Better than A$0.05 total loss

### Why Not Tighter?

- Stock is too illiquid to exit at any price
- Tight stops would trigger on normal volatility
- Mining stocks regularly have 50% drawdowns
